Know the Signs of Stroke: F.A.S.T.

Quick action can save lives. Recognize these signs and act immediately.

F

Face Drooping

One side of the face droops or feels numb. Ask them to smile.

A

Arm Weakness

One arm is weak or numb. Ask them to raise both arms.

S

Speech Difficulty

Speech is slurred or strange. Ask them to repeat a simple phrase.

T

Time to Call

If you see any of these signs, call emergency services immediately.
