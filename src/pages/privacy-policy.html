Privacy Notice

The Metro Vigan Hospital respects your right to privacy and knows that your health information is sensitive and confidential. We are committed to protecting you and your personal data.

As our patient, we will be making a medical record with your health information, including the care and treatment you receive. So that we can best meet your medical needs, we must share your medical record with the health care providers involved in your care. We also share your information only to the extent necessary to collect payment for the services we provide to you, and to comply with the laws that govern health care. We may also use medical information to review our treatment and services, as well as to evaluate the performance of our staff in caring for you. We will not use or disclose your information for any other purpose without your permission.

In addition to your healthcare team, our hospital personnel will also have access to your records to contact you for appointments or to provide you with assistance while you are in the hospital. They share MVH's commitment to protect your personal data.

The Hospital complies with the Data Privacy Act and adheres to data privacy principles. We uphold your rights as data subjects and we have implemented security measures to safeguard your information. Should you require more information, we shall be happy to assist you. If you believe your privacy rights have been violated, you may raise your concerns with our Data Protection Officer, (Orlino V. Abalos, via email at bounce_capt@yahoo.com or contact 09275090414.
